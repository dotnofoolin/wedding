Background Information

Josh was a member of FFA in High School and participated in the Gifted and Talented program. His interests include cars, computers, and everything else. Basically, Josh is a jack of all trades. Josh is a Computer Science/Math major at Henderson State University and will graduate in December 2003. He currently works for Ezclick.net in Arkadelphia as a Computer Technician.

Ashley Ponder was raised In Fordyce, AR. She is the daughter of Auburn Ponder and the late Ted Ponder and has one brother, Kelly Ponder. Ashley was active in high school and continued that in college as the editor of the Oracle, HSU's newspaper, for one year. She is a Psychology major/Sociology minor at HSU and will graduate in May 2003. She currently works for Group Living, Inc. helping developmentally disabled individuals with their everyday needs. Ashley will return back to school in the spring of 2004 to earn a graduate degree in community counseling.

Love was in the Air

Josh and Ashley met at Henderson State University their freshman year. Ashley had just moved away from home and was very excited about her newfound freedom. Josh commuted from Bismarck everyday to save money. They met their first semester in English class, but did not start dating until the following semester. They got to know each other first and went on their first date February 3, 2000.

They had so much fun together and realized that they definitely wanted to always be together. Josh first told Ashley that he loved her on April 2, 2000. From then on, Ashley and Josh knew that they wanted to get married someday, but they wanted to be out of college first.

Well, a few months went by, and Christmas was fast approaching.  Josh knew that he wanted to spend the rest of his life with Ashley, so he splurged and got her a promise ring, and a promise that one day they would be married.

Many Christmas's, Valentine's Days, and Anniversaries happened, and the couple were still just as much in love as the first day that they knew the other was their soul-mate.

The Engagement

Josh and Ashley decided that they would take a trip to Eureka Springs. After two years and nine months of dating, the couple had never been on a fun trip together. So they started planning. This sounded like a great way to get away from all of the pressures of school. Ashley had a feeling that Josh might ask her to marry him on this weekend. He had been hinting around about how he would ask her, but he assured her that he did not have enough money to purchase a ring. But all of Ashley's friends had this feeling that she would get engaged that weekend. But Ashley put it out of her head and did not believe them.

So Josh and Ashley set out for Eureka Springs. It rained all weekend and was very chilly. The couple didn't do much sight-seeing Friday night,  but Saturday they set out on the town. Even though Ashley put the engagement idea out of her mind, she was still a little suspicious. So she decided to look in Josh's overnight bag. She felt around the clothes, being very careful not to disturb them. She did not find the ring, and she was very disappointed. She didn't tell Josh about looking in his bag, and they went on about their day. They went shopping, saw the Passion Play, ate at Cafe Luigi, and went on a horse and buggy ride.

\The ride was at the historic Crescent Hotel. So the couple went behind the hotel to a garden, and Josh pointed out a bench he wanted to sit on. Josh started talking about their first date and reminiscing. But Ashley was kind of oblivious to why he was saying all of this. Josh then stood up and started stretching with his hands in his jean pockets. Ashley inquired about what he was doing. He replied, "I'm stretching." Ashley wondered why Josh was stretching so long. Then Josh stopped stretching, got down on one knee and said "Ashley, will you marry me?" Ashley was speechless and very excited. She jumped up and down and much to her surprise, she had not answered him. She finally got out a yes, and the couple has been living in engagement bliss since then.
